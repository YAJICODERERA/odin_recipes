Mango Float Recipe

Description.

This is a Filipino classic dessert that everybody loves, and is such a need during today's very hot season!

Ingredients

  • 4-5 ripe mangoes, peeled and sliced
  • 2 cups heavy cream, chilled
  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pack (200 grams) graham crackers
  • 1/2 cup crushed graham crackers or nuts for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, whip the heavy cream until it forms stiff peaks. You can use a hand mixer or stand mixer for this step. Be careful not to overmix.
  2. Slowly pour the condensed milk into the whipped cream, and add the vanilla extract. Gently fold the mixture until well combined. This will be your cream mixture.
  3. In a rectangular dish or any serving container of your choice, arrange a layer of graham crackers at the bottom. You may need to break some crackers to fit the gaps.
  4. Spread a layer of the cream mixture over the graham crackers, making sure to cover them evenly.
  5. Arrange a layer of sliced mangoes on top of the cream mixture.
  6. Repeat the layers until you reach the desired height or until you run out of ingredients.
  7. Finish with a final layer of cream mixture on top.
  8. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow the flavors to meld and the dessert to set.
  9. Once chilled and set, slice and serve the mango float cold. Enjoy!
