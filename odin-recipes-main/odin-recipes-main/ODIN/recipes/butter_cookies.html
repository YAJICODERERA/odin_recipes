Homemade Butter Cookies Recipe

Description.

These butter cookies are simple yet delicious, perfect for any occasion or as a sweet treat with your afternoon tea or coffee.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt (optional)
  • Additional granulated sugar for rolling (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper or silicone baking mat.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. You can use a hand mixer or stand mixer for this step.
  3. Add the vanilla extract and mix until well combined.
  4. Gradually add the flour to the butter mixture, mixing until a dough forms. If desired, add a pinch of salt to enhance the flavor of the cookies.
  5. Shape the cookie dough into small balls, about 1 inch in diameter, and place them on the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each cookie.
  6. If you want to add extra sweetness and a decorative touch, you can roll each cookie ball in granulated sugar before placing them on the baking sheet.
  7. Use a fork to gently flatten each cookie ball, creating a crisscross pattern on top.
  8. Remove the cookies from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  9. Once cooled, store the butter cookies in an airtight container at room temperature. Enjoy!
